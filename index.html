Why Choose DogHaven?

Safety and Supervision

Dog coaches ensure all the lovely dogs are safe and well-supervised while they are enjoying their stay at DogHaven. We enforce the safety measures we have in place, such as secure fencing, trained staff, and surveillance, to give dog owners peace of mind.

Play and Socialization

DogHaven offers various fun and engaging activities that fits to all lovely dogs and a playground suitable both young and adult dogs to ensure that the dog the excercise and interactions they need.
